光線好, 咖啡慢。

Good light, slow coffee.

Open Wed–Mon · 10:00–18:00週三至週一 10:00–18:00 Yancheng · 2 min from Pier-2鹽埕・駁二步行兩分鐘

We took over grandma's dry-goods shop in 2019 and never really left. The roaster lives out back, brunch comes off a charcoal grill, and the fishermen still argue at the window seat. Everyone gets the same careful cup.

2019 年,我們接手了阿嬤的南北貨老店,就再也沒離開。烘豆機住在後院,早午餐從炭爐上來,窗邊的漁伯照樣邊喝邊聊。每一杯,我們都一樣認真。
