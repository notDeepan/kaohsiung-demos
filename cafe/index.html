Our story關於我們

A sixty-year-old shophouse,
full of coffee.

六十年老屋裡的咖啡香。

01 — the house老屋

We took over grandma's dry-goods shop and never really left.

我們接手了阿嬤的南北貨老店,然後再也沒離開。

In 2019 we moved a roaster, a brew bar and the smell of butter toast into the old shelves. On most mornings you'll find harbor fishermen next to travelers with cameras — everyone gets the same careful cup.

2019 年,我們把烘豆機、手沖吧台和奶油吐司的香氣,搬進老貨架之間。清晨的店裡,常常是港邊漁人和背相機的旅人並肩而坐——每一杯,我們都一樣認真。

English menu, English-speaking staff, outlets at every seat. Come as you are.

全店英文菜單、店員可英文點餐,每個座位都有插座。隨時歡迎。

— Ah-Hui & Min, owners店主 阿輝與敏
