Getting here交通

Ten minutes from the city, a world away

離市區十分鐘,像另一個世界

MRT to Sizihwan (O1)捷運到「西子灣站」(O1)

Orange Line, Exit 1. From Kaohsiung Main Station it's about 15 minutes.

橘線 1 號出口,從高雄車站出發約 15 分鐘。

Gushan Ferry → Cijin鼓山輪渡站 → 旗津

Boats every 5–10 minutes in the daytime, about NT$40, EasyCard OK. Bikes and scooters ride along.

白天每 5–10 分鐘一班,約 NT$40,可刷一卡通/悠遊卡,單車機車都能上船。
