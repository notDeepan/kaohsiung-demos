Our story品牌故事

From Dashu farms to your suitcase

從大樹果園,到你的行李箱

We're a two-person team from Kaohsiung. Every season we buy directly from five family farms in Dashu and Qishan — golden-diamond pineapples, Yuhebao lychees, red guavas — and dry them slowly at low heat. No added sugar, no preservatives, nothing to hide.

我們是來自高雄的兩人小團隊。每一季直接向大樹、旗山的五戶果農採購——金鑽鳳梨、玉荷包荔枝、紅心芭樂——低溫慢烘,不加糖、無防腐劑,沒什麼好隱瞞的。

You met us at the night market or the airport. Now we can come to you.

你或許在夜市或機場遇過我們。現在,換我們寄到你家。
